Our vision

TO BE THE MOST
CUSTOMER CENTRIC COMPANY IN INDIA




Borosil is India's most trusted glassware brand since 1962. Synonymous with heatproof glassware, it is the market leader for consumer glassware in India.



Over half a century of expertise in producing solar glass, speciality glass including rolled glass is what makes Borosil Renewables Limited an invaluable member of the Borosil group.



Klasspack Pvt. Ltd. is one of India's leading manufactures of Glass Ampoules and Tubular Glass Vials, widely used as primary packaging materials by pharmaceutical companies for their life-saving drugs.



BOROSIL TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED in Pune is a Borosil entity mandated to research, design, develop and introduce laboratory equipment under the LabQuest brand that are smart, rugged & practical.
